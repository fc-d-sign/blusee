Given the charisma and unique nature of the term “VOX” in the name of the company, the word itself was chosen as the main element of VOX – Organização Gráfica Industrial S.A.’s logotype. By fusing the letter “X” with the crop marks symbol used in printing and tainting it with the four elementary colours, cyan, magenta, yellow and black, the brand was able to explicitly evince its business field. Moreover, it was important to correctly display the colours not only to empower the CMYK system concept, but also to induce a rotative perception of the letter, resembling the motion of an offset cylinder. Ultimately, those colours would then be used in the company’s personnel business cards to distinguish different departments of the company (ex.: human resources, finance, creative and production departments), earning this way practical functions other than their usual decorative applications.

The aim of this project was to create an insignia for the comic bookstore just as a lot of the superheroes within those comic books possess. Therefore, the initial letter “M” was fused with the very concepts portrayed by the bookstore’s name, Mundo Fantasma (Ghost World). Firstly, the symbol was moulded into an ellipse to not only resemble the shape of a planet and definition of the “world” word, but also the one of a classic speech bubble. Additionally, that very same shape is filled with gapped coloured bands, giving the logotype its righteous colourful essence and creating a phantasmagorical visual effect that would then be extended to the whole brand. Lastly, the lettering is also aimed to contrast both mentioned concepts by using different weights on each word.
